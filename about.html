ABOUT US


We aim to change the status quo. We aim to think differently. We do so by telling compelling stories in the most immersive manner possible, and hosting them on products that are user friendly, beautifully designed, and easy to use. We are Jiva: pioneers in Virtual Reality Direct Marketing (VRDM).

WHAT WE DO


Jiva is a virtual reality content creation and distribution agency. Our end-to-end 360 video services make it possible for any demographic to enjoy exceptional virtual reality content at the push of a button.

CONCEPTUALIZATION


VR is new. It’s exciting and innovative. That’s why you are here; to be at the forefront of technology and communicate in a unique way that deeply resonates. In order to deliver, you need a team dedicated to helping you work through this complex technology. In this initial phase, Jiva will deploy its creative and technological expertise to cultivate your vision and develop a step-by-step plan to execute.

FIELD


Jiva and its extended network of VR A/V talent is comprised of some of the most accomplished immersive storytellers in the world. Our team's cameras and audio equipment have placed viewers on top of Everest, into the depths of the ocean, inside mind-bending waves and captured corporate environments in the most progressive manner possible. No matter the challenge, environment, or scale, we will bring your message to life.

Stitching, editing, color grading and sound design: this is where the bulk of VR production lies, and requires the greatest attention to detail. Jiva goes to great lengths to ensure all final deliverables are completed to the highest standard, even if it means not sleeping for a while.

DISTRIBUTION


Content is pre-loaded onto fully customizable headsets with numerous options for branding both device and packaging. The end result is a powerful business tool offering unlimited possibilities for product demonstration, education, training or entertainment.
